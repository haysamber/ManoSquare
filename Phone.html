Getting Real: A Chat Game


Talk to a friend

Experience the power to enable or disable toxic thinking patterns in this RPG chat game.

  • Created by Charlotte Ruth.

    • About: A choose-your-own-story game where you play as Issac talking to your struggling friend, Simon. Your responses shape how he reacts, if you send supportive messages, he opens up while sending negative ones can make Simon more distant. The chat background shifts colors based on the tone of your choices.
