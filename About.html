About The ManoSquare:

These three mini-games explore the consequences and interactions of internet subcultures, mental illness and male loneliness. The manosphere is a modern social term to define online spaces that uphold misogynistic or strong gender norms. These social expectations don't just oppress women, but they teach men to keep each other down as well.

Our job is to help you understand the challenge of existing and growing up in these spaces. True internal and social change comes easier with understanding, not blind judgement, and our team of developers and artists want to help the world begin to understand the struggle.
